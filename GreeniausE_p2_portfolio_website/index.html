edge designs

edge designs is the portfolio website for Emelie Greeniaus, a passionate and versatile designer with a keen eye for detail and a dedication to creating impactful, user-centred designs.

Specializing in both digital and print media, Emelie combines creativity with functionality to craft visually stunning solutions that resonate with clients and users alike. The site showcases a curated collection of her work across various industries, highlighting her skills in branding, poster design, illustration, and more.

Whether you're looking for innovative design concepts or thoughtful branding strategies, Edge Designs offers a glimpse into Emelie's ability to turn ideas into compelling visual narratives.
