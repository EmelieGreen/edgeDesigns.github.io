About

Hi! My name is Emelie Greeniaus

I am a 20-year-old college student and aspiring graphic designer with a deep passion for both digital and tactile creation. My work spans beyond the Adobe Suite, encompassing a diverse range of materials such as resin, paint, paper, vinyl, wax, and more. This fusion of traditional and digital mediums allows me to explore new possibilities in design and push the boundaries of my creativity. Outside of design, I am an avid reader and a lover of television, drawing inspiration from both worlds. As reflected in the vibrant aesthetic of this website, my favourite colours are pink and green, which evoke a sense of playfulness and harmony that resonates throughout my work.
